Welcome to Sunnah Circle!

A Canadian organization which provides Islamic education on-site and online for ladies, kids and young boys and girls. Supervised by authentic Islamic scholars who are well-grounded in a tradition based on the Qur’an and the Sunnah of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ as understood by the Sahabah (R.A) and the Ulama.

Our Mission

Established in 2007 under the former name, Pearls of Islam, Sunnah Circle has many opportunities where you and your community can gain access to exclusive education and learn more about Islam. As the youth and many others are confronted by many hardships and fitnas, we are here to provide you with authentic Islamic education and aid, from on-site to online.

Our mission is to provide Islamic teachings by creating environment where students can nurture and strengthen their Imaan as they have the right to Islamic education. We want the students to obtain Islamic education from certified teachers so that sacred Ilm (Knowledge) is passed down through a credible link.

Our vision is to educate and guide muslims all around the world using modern technology and tools. Learning through the teachings of the Qur’an and Sunnah and following the footsteps of beloved companions of our Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, in their daily lives and becoming role-models for their families and communities.

We at Sunnah Circle established this platform to provide relevant and inspirational Islamic learning experience to the global community.
