In the name of Allah. The Most Beneficent, the Most Merciful, and peace and blessings upon His final messenger,Muhammad ﷺ, and upon his Family and Companions.

Welcome to Sunnah Circle!

We are a Canada-based organization which provides Islamic education on-site and online for Ladies, kids and young boys and girls. We are under the supervision of authentic Islamic scholars who are well-grounded in a tradition based on the Qur’an and the Sunnah of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ as understood by the Sahabah (R.A) and the Ulama.

Our Mission

Established in 2007 under the former name, Pearls of Islam, Sunnah Circle has many opportunities where your child(ren) can get educated and learn more about our Deen and Islam. As you know, there are many fitnas in this world, so we are here to provide you with authentic Islamic education on-site and online

Our mission is to provide Islamic teachings and we believe in the growth and development of an Islamic environment where students strengthen their Emaan and have the right Islamic education. We want the students to get Islamic education from knowledgeable teachers and to make sure the students know what the importance of Emaan is and have a strong moral character.

Our vision is to ensure that an Islamic environment exists here at Sunnah Circle, according to the Qur’an and Sunnah and following the footsteps of beloved companions of our Prophet Muhammad ﷺ . Our goal is that our future generations should be well educated and knowledgeable Muslims who fear Allah and apply the Sunnah and teachings of the Prophet Muhammad (s.a.w) in their daily lives and become better Muslims as well as role-models for their families and communities.

We at Sunnah Circle established this platform to provide relevant and inspirational Islamic learning experience to the global community.
