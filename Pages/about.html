OUR ORIGINS & ORGANIZATION

History

Pearls of Islam

Pearls of Islam, established in 2007 by Umme Zaki, was as an online Islamic bookstore, providing Islamic studies and online Tajweed classes for women only. Pearls of Islam had suspended activity for some time and has now resumed under the name Sunnah Circle.

Sunnah Circle

Upon rebranding, Sunnah Circle has been built upon the firm belief of the Ahl-Sunnah Wal Jama'ah and all our instructors are graduates or students of classical islamic learning (traditional madaaris).

Our curriculum is based on the Hanafi fiqh.

We are under the supervision of authentic Islamic scholars who are well-grounded in a tradition based on the Qur’an and the Sunnah of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ as understood by the Sahabah (R.A) and the Ulama.

President - Ustaadha Umme Zaki

Our respected Instructor (Ustaadha) Umme Zaki, currently lives in Canada. She is very passionate about teaching Qur’an and Islamic studies with experience in tutoring students of all age groups, spanning for over 15 years. Ustaadha Umme Zaki is currently a teacher for Qur’an and Islamic Studies at Taha Musallah Madrissah located in Brampton, ON, Canada while running her own local institute called Sunnah Circle for kids and young girls.

Ustaadha Umme Zaki has also previously taught Qur’an, Qa’idah, and Islamic Studies at Al Suffah Academy, and Ummati Islamic School. Ustaadha has also been a faculty member at Qur’an Academy Online and Ar Rahman Islamic Center with experience as an admin for an online radio channel called Defenders of Truth from 2003 to 2007. She is also one of the founding members of Fahmul-Quran and worked as an administrator from 2007 to 2012.

Majoring in Psychology, Ustaadha graduated from Pakistan which was followed by diplomas in Qur’anic Translation and Tafsir, Tajweed, Arabic, and Hadith from Islamic Institute of Sciences in Canada. Ustadha has also remained a disciple at the prestigious Jamia Binoria where continued to pursue Qur’anic Tafsir, Hadith, Fiqh, Arabic Grammar, and Tajweed. She has also studied Tajweed from Jamia Ashrafia Online and Qur’an Academy, Pakistan.

Ustadha is currently in her final year of the A'allimah program at the Zainab Academy Online. Additionally, Ustaadha works as the teaching assistant for Zainab Academy Online for Hidayahtun Nahw. She is learning Sciences of Qirat and different riwayah. Ustaadha is also doing Takhasus in Tajweed from Noor ul Huda academy. Ustadha is also teaches Arabic grammer to beginners.

May Allah reward her efforts, and allow it to be accepted as a sadqa-e-jaariah. Ameen.

Vice President - Ustaadha Umme Talha (Shaista Mukhtar)

Umme Talha has obtained her Tajweed education from a variety of Pakistani and Arab teachers. She has been a teacher of recitation of Qur’an Al Kareem with Tajweed since 2006. She has also helped students memorize surahs of the Qur’an. She is currently a student at Al-Otrojah.

She is the founder of Jamiah Zafar Noraniyah, Jamiah Zahida and Jamiah Sultana, where she teaches Tajweed to ladies.

She has been engaged in Doura e Quran programs and Halaqas for women in the community since 2008. She is in her final year of the Alimah program at Zaynab Academy Online. During the past 5 years of this course, she has contributed as a Teacher Assistant for multiple subjects such as, Quranic Tafseer, Tajweed, Hadith, and Siraji (Laws of inheritance/wiraasat).

She has a son and a daughter. She has been living in Canada for over 15 years. She also has a Bachelors degree of Arts from Pakistan.
