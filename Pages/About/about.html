Our History and Founder

A relevant, authentic and inspirational Islamic learning experience for the global community.

History

Pearls of Islam

Pearls of Islam was established in 2007, by Umme Zaki, as an online Islamic bookstore, and providing Islamic studies and online Tajweed classes for women only. Due to some issues, Pearls of Islam was closed for a few years and now has started again with a new name Sunnah Circle.

Sunnah Circle

Upon rebranding, Sunnah Circle has been built upon the firm belief of the Ahl al-Sunnah wal-Jama’ah and all our instructors are followers to the famous Islamic school of laws named after their respective imams: Imam Abu Hanifah Nu’man ibn Thabit, Imam Malik ibn Anas, Imam Muhammad ibn Idris al-Shafi’i and Imam Ahmad ibn Hanbal (may Allah have mercy on all of them). Our curriculum is based on the Hanafi fiqh.

We are under the supervision of authentic Islamic scholars who are well-grounded in a tradition based on the Qur’an and the Sunnah of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ as understood by the Sahabah (R.A) and the Ulama.

Our Founder - Ustaadha Umme Zaki

Our respected Professor (Ustaadha) Umme Zaki, currently lives in Canada. She is very passionate about teaching Qur’an and Islamic Studies. She has an experience of over 15 years of tutoring students of all age groups. She currently teaches Qur’an and Islamic Studies at Taha Musallah Madrissah in Canada and also runs her own local institute called Sunnah Circle for kids and young girls. She previously taught Qur’an at Al Suffah Academy, as well as

Qur’an, Qa’idah, and Islamic Studies at Ummati Islamic School. Ustadha has also been a faculty member at Qur’an Academy Online and Ar Rahman Islamic Center and has also worked as an admin in an online radio channel called Defenders of Truth from 2003 to 2007. She is also one of the founding members of Fahmul-Quran and worked as an administrator from 2007 to 2012.

She graduated in Psychology from Pakistan. She has a diploma in Qur’anic Translation and Tafsir, alongside Tajweed, Arabic, and Hadith from Islamic Institute of Sciences in Canada. Ustadha has also remained a disciple at the prestigious Jamia Binoria where she studied Qur’anic Tafsir, Hadith, Fiqh, Arabic Grammar, and Tajwid. She has also studied Tajweed from Jamia Ashrafia Online and Qur’an Academy, Pakistan.

Ustadha is on her way to becoming an ’Alimah as well, she is currently doing her Daurae Hadith as she is in Year 05 of the Aalimiyyah Programme. Alongside, at the same institute, she works as the teaching assistant for Hidayah tun Nahv.She is also learing Siince of Qeraat and Difrent rewayaat.ustadh is also doing Takhasus in Tajweed from Noor ul Huda academy.Ustadha is also Teacher Arabic grammer for biganers.
